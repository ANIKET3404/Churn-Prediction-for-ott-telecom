CHURN PREDICTION

Churn Prediction

"Churn prediction and prevention for OTT/Telecommunication" refers to the process of predicting and preventing customer churn within the context of Over-The-Top (OTT) or Telecommunication services.

The goal of churn prediction is to develop models or algorithms that can accurately predict which customers are likely to churn in the future. This can be done by analyzing various customer-related factors such as usage patterns, subscription details, customer demographics, complaints, customer service interactions, and other relevant data. Once churn prediction models are developed, preventive measures can be taken to retain customers and reduce churn. These measures can include targeted marketing campaigns, personalized offers, improved customer service, loyalty programs, and proactive customer engagement strategies.

Churn Prevention

Churn prevention refers to the set of strategies, actions, and measures taken by businesses to reduce customer churn. Customer churn, also known as customer attrition or customer turnover, occurs when customers discontinue using a product or service and switch to a competitor or simply stop using the product/service altogether.

Churn prevention focuses on retaining existing customers and minimizing the number of customers who leave or unsubscribe from a product or service. This is important because acquiring new customers can be more costly and challenging compared to retaining existing ones.Customer retention programs: Implementing loyalty programs, special offers, discounts, or rewards to incentivize customers to stay and continue using the product or service.
